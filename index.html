Water Quality Analytics Dashboard

The dashboards provide an overview of water quality by showing the degree of water contamination in various water sources in the selected districts. The water sources are made up of protected springs, shallow wells, tanks, taps, and boreholes. The water sources are located in sixteen districts from central, eastern, western and northern regions of Uganda. I-WAQ documentation
Stacked Bar Chart
The stacked bar chart provides a visual representation of the levels of Potassium, Nitrates, Phosphates, Ammonia, Nitrites, Fluoride, Turbidity, and pH in the water sources. Stacked Bar Chart
